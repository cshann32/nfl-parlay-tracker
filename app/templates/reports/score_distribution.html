How to use this for Over/Under betting
The histogram above shows how often games land in each 5-point scoring bucket. If the sportsbook sets an O/U at 47.5, count what % of games historically land above vs. below that line. The average total and median are your baseline anchors. The home PPG vs. away PPG gap tells you how much home-field affects scoring (typically 2–3 points). The weekly trend line shows which parts of the season tend to run higher or lower.
